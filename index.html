Yang Hou

PhD Student, School of Computer Science and Technology, Soochow University
I am a PhD student at Soochow University, advised by Prof. Zhenghua Li. My research focuses on core natural language processing, especially syntactic and semantic structure modeling, including constituency parsing, dependency parsing, semantic role labeling, and large language models for structured linguistic analysis.
More broadly, I am interested in how linguistic structure can be modeled, induced, and exploited in neural systems, and how large language models can be evaluated and improved for syntax-related tasks.
